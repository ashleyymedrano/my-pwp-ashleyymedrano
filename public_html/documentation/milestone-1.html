Personal Website Project: Ashley Medrano

Purpose:

  • I would like my personal website project to help me accomplish a visual and dynamic presentation of my professional journey, skills, and accomplishments to potential employers. I aim to showcase not only my technical skills and creative abilities but also my dedication web development. By creating this project, I aspire to attract opportunities that align with my career aspirations as well as being able to connect with people who have the same aligned goals.

Audience

  • The people who would be using or viewing my site are employers, recruiters, peers and colleagues, and even the public. This audience is who the PWP is directed to as these are the professionals in the work setting who I would like to connect with. I would also like it to be user-friendly for employers and recruiters who would view the website as well.

Goal

  • My overall goal for the project is to showcase my work to employers and recruiters to land an internship, part-time, and full-time employment. Also, I would like my website to serve as a gallery for my projects and accomplishments while also having it be a place to connect and network with peers and colleagues.
